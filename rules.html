TalkRush — Community Rules & Guidelines

You can chat in English, हिन्दी (Hindi), தமிழ் (Tamil), বাংলা (Bangla), मराठी (Marathi), తెలుగు (Telugu), ગુજરાતી (Gujarati), ਪੰਜਾਬੀ (Punjabi), اُردُو (Urdu), and other Indian & international languages.

Quick Principles

  • Be kind and respectful — no harassment, bullying, hate, or threats.
  • Keep TalkRush safe for everyone — no sexual content with minors, explicit nudity, graphic violence, or illegal content.
  • Images & GIFs are allowed. Share only safe, non‑NSFW, non‑gory media you have rights to post.
  • Multilingual chats are welcome. Switch languages if someone asks politely.
  • Protect privacy. Don’t share personal or confidential info (yours or others’).
  • No spam, scams, or self‑promotion blasts. No bots or automation without permission.
🔞 You must be 18+ or the age of majority in your region to use TalkRush.
🛡️ Break the rules ⇒ warnings, mutes, or bans.

Allowed on TalkRush ✅

  • Friendly conversations in any language (use translators if needed).
  • Sharing safe images and GIFs related to the chat topic.
  • Creative, educational, tech, gaming, sports, art, and general discussion.
  • Respectful debate. Attack ideas, not people.

Use With Care

  • Dark humor or sensitive topics: add content warnings and stop if asked.
  • Religious/political talk: keep it civil; no proselytizing or hate.

Not Allowed ❌

  • Harassment, hate speech, slurs, sexism, racism, casteism, ableism, or violent threats.
  • Sexual content, explicit nudity, fetish content, or sexual discussions with/depicting minors.
  • Gore, shocking/graphic media, or content that glorifies self‑harm or suicide.
  • Illegal activities (drugs, weapons, fraud, hacking, piracy) or instructions to commit them.
  • Doxxing or sharing private data (names, phones, addresses, IDs, social links) without consent.
  • Impersonation, deepfakes, defamation, or targeted harassment.
  • Spam, link farming, repetitive promos, or scams.
  • Malware, phishing, or attempts to bypass platform security.
  • Copyright infringement or posting images/GIFs you don’t have rights to share.

Images & GIFs 📷

  • Only share images/GIFs that are safe for work and non‑graphic.
  • Blur or avoid sensitive content; add a clear [CW] label when needed.
  • Never upload others’ private photos or any content taken/used without consent.
  • Strictly no nudity, sexual content, or sexualized minors (instant ban).
  • Report media that violates these rules using the in‑app report tools.

Language & Culture 🌏

  • All Indian & international languages are welcome. Be patient with learners.
  • If someone doesn’t understand, try simpler words or switch languages.
  • Avoid insults or stereotypes about regions, religions, or communities.

Privacy & Safety 🔐

  • Don’t share: your exact location, passwords/OTP, financial details, or private IDs.
  • Think before you post. Public chats may be visible to others and stored for moderation.
  • We may keep logs to enforce rules and improve safety.
  • Use built‑in tools to block, mute, or report users who break rules.

Spam, Ads & Automation 🧹

  • No mass‑messaging, repetitive links, or unsolicited promotions.
  • No scraping or auto‑starting chats; bots and extensions need written approval.
  • Affiliate/partner links must be clearly disclosed and permitted by TalkRush.

Enforcement & Appeals ⚖️

  • We use progressive enforcement: reminder → warning → temporary mute/ban → permanent ban (for severe/repeat violations, we may skip steps).
  • Severe violations (e.g., sexual content with minors, threats, doxxing, malware) result in immediate permanent bans and reports to relevant authorities.
  • If you think we made a mistake, you can appeal via the in‑app help or support email with context and message links.

Legal & Regional Compliance 📜

  • Follow the laws of your country/region. When in doubt, don’t post.
  • By using TalkRush, you agree to these Rules, our Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy.
  • Local requirements may apply in India and other jurisdictions; we enforce the stricter standard where needed to keep users safe.

Need Help? 🆘

  • Use the Report or Block options on any message/user that breaks the rules.
  • Emergency risk (self‑harm, violence)? Contact local authorities or helplines immediately.
  • For support, include screenshots/links and your username so we can investigate.

Thanks for helping make TalkRush welcoming for everyone. Be kind, stay safe, and have great conversations! 💬
