Respect All Members

  • Treat all community members with kindness and respect
  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech of any kind
  • Be inclusive and welcoming to newcomers
  • Respect differences of opinion

Content Policy

ALLOWED Constructive discussions, helpful content, and appropriate sharing of resources
NOT ALLOWED Spam, self-promotion, explicit content, or any illegal material

Privacy & Safety

  • Do not share personal information of yourself or others
  • Report any suspicious activity to moderators immediately
  • Respect the privacy of all community members
  • Keep conversations appropriate for all ages

Code of Conduct

1. Be respectful and professional in all interactions
2. No flaming, trolling, or inflammatory comments
3. Stay on topic in discussions
4. Use appropriate language for a professional community
5. Respect the decisions of moderators
