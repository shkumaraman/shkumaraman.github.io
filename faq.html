    Yes, with easy search functionality.

    Yes, you can switch between dark and light mode.

  • 5. Can I copy and paste code?

    Yes, it supports easy copy-pasting.

  • 6. Can I share my code with others?

    Yes, generate a shareable link for collaboration.

  • 7. Are there any shortcuts or snippets?

    Yes, shortcut snippets and comments are available.
