Private Photo Sharing – Auto Delete in 24 Hours

Upload your photos securely and receive a unique 5-digit code. Share this code with your friends or send it via WhatsApp. Only the person who enters the code can view your photos. For your privacy, photos will be automatically deleted after 24 hours.

How It Works

  1. Upload – Choose up to 10 photos from your device. No sign-up or registration required.
  2. Get a Code – A unique 5-digit code is generated instantly (e.g., 48291).
  3. Share – Copy the code or send it directly via WhatsApp or any other messenger.
  4. View – Only people with the code can see your photos.
  5. Auto-Delete – Your photos will be permanently removed after 24 hours.

Why Choose This Service?

  • No account or personal information required
  • Fast and easy photo sharing
  • Secure and private – you control who sees your photos
  • Automatic deletion after 24 hours for extra privacy
  • Works on any device – phone, tablet, or computer

Note: Share your code only with people you trust. We never store your photos beyond 24 hours.
