About TalkRush

TalkRush is a platform where you can express yourself freely and connect with others around the world. You can:

  • Post anonymously and share your thoughts without revealing your identity
  • Chat with strangers safely and securely
  • Engage in real, open, and respectful conversations

Our goal is to create a safe space for honest expression and human connection — where everyone feels heard.

TalkRush supports multiple languages: You can chat in English, हिन्दी (Hindi), தமிழ் (Tamil), বাংলা (Bangla), मराठी (Marathi), తెలుగు (Telugu), ગુજરાતી (Gujarati), नेपाली (Nepali), ਪੰਜਾਬੀ (Punjabi), اُردُو (Urdu), and other Indian & international languages.
