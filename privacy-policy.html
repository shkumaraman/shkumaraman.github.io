Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TalkRush. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

Important: We do NOT collect any personal data from strangers or random users on the platform.

  • Personal Information: Only information that you voluntarily provide (such as your name or email). We do not collect personal data from unknown users.
  • Usage Data: Basic non-identifiable analytics such as IP, browser type, and device info.
  • Cookies: Used only for preferences, analytics, and improving user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, improve, and personalize our services.
  • To ensure user safety and apply basic moderation.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements if needed.

4. Data Retention Policy

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or retain any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or account details.
  • No User Accounts: Since our website does not provide account registration or login features, no user data is saved in our system.
  • Automatic Non-Retention: Any temporary technical data (such as basic server logs) is automatically removed or managed by our hosting provider and is not stored or accessed by us.
  • No Data Retention: Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information stored that requires retention or deletion.

5. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to show ads. Google may use cookies and tracking for personalized ads. You can control your preferences here: Google Ads Settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use essential third-party services such as analytics or ad networks. These services handle data under their own privacy policies.

7. Moderation Rules

For user safety, we follow basic moderation guidelines. You can read the full rules here:

View Rules & Moderation Policy

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region (GDPR/CCPA), you may have rights to access, delete, correct, or restrict your personal data.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be displayed on this page with the revised date mentioned at the top.
