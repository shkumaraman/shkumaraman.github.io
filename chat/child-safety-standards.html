TalkRush Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Preventing Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Zero Tolerance for CSAE

TalkRush is a **strictly 18+ platform**, and we have **zero tolerance** for any form of **Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)**. We are committed to keeping our community safe and ensuring that our platform is free from any illegal activities.

Safety Measures Implemented

  • Strict Age Verification: Users must confirm they are **18+** before accessing the app.
  • AI-Based Moderation: Content filtering to detect and prevent any CSAE-related content.
  • User Reporting System: Report buttons available for users to flag inappropriate behavior.
  • Automated Bans: Any user violating our safety standards is **permanently banned**.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We work with authorities to report and remove offenders.
