Private 1v1 Chat – Invite a Friend & Connect Instantly

Create a private room and invite exactly who you want. No random strangers — just you and the person you choose. Share a room code or a direct invite link and start chatting in a secure, private space.

How Private Chat Rooms Work

Click "Create Room" to instantly generate a unique 5-digit room code. Share that code or the invite link with anyone you want to talk to. Once they join, you two are connected — no one else can enter. Each room holds exactly 2 participants.

Already have a code? Just enter it in "Join Room" and you'll connect immediately. Opening an invite link in a browser takes you straight into the room automatically.

Features of Private 1v1 Chat

  • Video Calling – Start a live video call with one click. Toggle your camera and microphone anytime during the call.
  • Image Sharing – Send photos directly in chat without any third-party app or upload service.
  • GIF Support – Browse trending GIFs or search GIPHY to find the perfect reaction and send it instantly.
  • Easy Room Sharing – Use the Share button to copy or share your invite link directly from the app.

Why Use a Private Room Instead of Random Chat

Random chat is great for meeting new people, but sometimes you just want to talk to someone specific. A private 1v1 room gives you a dedicated space with a person you already know or want to reconnect with — no waiting queue, no unexpected strangers.

Whether you're catching up with a friend, having a confidential conversation, or just want a distraction-free chat with someone you trust, the private room keeps things focused and secure.

Is TalkRush Private Chat Secure

Yes. All communication happens directly between the two participants using peer-to-peer (WebRTC) technology — messages and video never pass through a central server. Your room automatically closes when either participant leaves, and no chat history is stored.

Never share your room code publicly if you want to keep the conversation private. Rooms support a maximum of 2 people, so no one can silently join in the background.

Popular Uses for Private 1v1 Chat

People use private rooms for quick check-ins with friends, language exchange practice with a study partner, online interviews, remote tutoring sessions, or simply having a longer and more personal conversation away from group noise. The combination of text, images, GIFs, and video makes it a complete communication tool without any sign-up or installation.
