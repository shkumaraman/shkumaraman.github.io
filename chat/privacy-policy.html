Welcome to TalkRush. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information, particularly when displaying advertisements through third-party services.

2. Information We Collect

TalkRush does not directly collect personal data from users. However, our app may display advertisements using Google AdMob, which may collect and process certain information to serve relevant ads.

3. Third-Party Services

We integrate Google AdMob to provide advertisements. AdMob may collect the following types of data:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique identifiers.
  • Location Data: If permitted by the user, approximate location data may be used for ad personalization.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Information on ad impressions, clicks, and engagement.

For further details on how Google AdMob processes user data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Managing Your Privacy

Users can manage their ad preferences through Google by visiting Google Ad Settings. Additionally, users can opt out of personalized ads via their device settings.

5. Security Measures

We prioritize the security of our users. While we do not store personal data, we follow best industry practices to ensure a secure app experience. However, since advertisements are managed by Google AdMob, we encourage users to review their privacy policies for full transparency.
