1. Overview

Thank you for using TalkRush. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle user information, particularly in relation to app permissions and advertisements served through third-party platforms.

TalkRush collects limited personal information such as your name and email address when you voluntarily provide it (e.g., during sign-up or feedback submission). We do not collect sensitive personal data.

For more information, refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

4. User Control

You may:

We do not store sensitive personal user data. All third-party integrations are reviewed to ensure compliance with industry standards for privacy and security.
