Privacy Policy for TalkRush

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

TalkRush collects minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Basic Account Information: When you register, we collect your name and email address.
  • App Permissions: The app may request:
    • File storage access (to save/retrieve user-generated content)
    • Microphone access (only when using audio features, with explicit user consent)
  • Automatically Collected Data: Non-personal information about device type, OS version, and app usage statistics.

2. Advertising and Analytics

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements, which may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address (for approximate regional targeting)
  • Interaction data with ads

This data helps serve relevant ads while protecting user privacy. You can manage preferences via Google's Ad Settings.

3. What We Don't Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Precise GPS location data
  • Contact lists or personal files
  • Sensitive device information

4. Data Usage

Collected information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Improve user experience
  • Display contextual advertisements
  • Respond to user inquiries

5. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Service Providers: Only for essential operational purposes

We never sell user data to third parties.

6. User Controls

You have full control over:

  • App permissions (can be denied or revoked anytime via device settings)
  • Advertising preferences (via device settings or Google's controls)
  • Account information (editable within the app)

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures including:

  • Limited data collection
  • Secure transmission protocols
  • Regular security reviews

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. Significant changes will be notified through the app.
