About this app

Connect instantly with strangers worldwide through text, voice, video chats, group chats and Stories. TalkRush is the fastest way to meet interesting people and make new friends.

Features

  • Multiple Chat Options - Text chat, voice calls, and video calls with strangers
  • Group Chats - Join existing group conversations with multiple people at once
  • Stories Feature NEW - Share your daily moments that disappear after 24 hours
  • Social Feed NEW - Post images and text in the feed. Like, comment, reply and share posts with other users
  • Group Chat Rooms - Join topic-based rooms to meet people with similar interests
  • View Stories - See what others are sharing and connect through shared interests
  • Advanced Filters - Find matches by gender, location, and interests
  • Story Reactions - React to stories with emojis to start conversations
  • Easy Signup - Register with just email and password
  • Safe Environment - AI-powered moderation and reporting system
