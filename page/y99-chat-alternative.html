Safety Tips for Online Chat Rooms and Stranger Chat

Any website that lets you talk to strangers should be used carefully. Online chat can be fun, but users should protect their privacy and avoid risky behavior.

Important Stranger Chat Safety Rules

Do not share your phone number, address, school, workplace, passwords, financial details, private photos, or social media accounts with strangers. Use a nickname instead of your real name. Leave any conversation that feels uncomfortable. Do not trust links sent by unknown users. Keep video chat respectful and avoid showing personal information in your background.

If you are unsure about someone, stay in public rooms first. Move to private rooms only when you feel comfortable. If someone pressures you, asks for personal details, sends suspicious links, or behaves badly, leave the chat.
