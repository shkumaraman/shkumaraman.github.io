PlyGram Account Deletion Request

We value your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. If you wish to delete your PlyGram account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Option 1: Delete via the PlyGram App

You can delete your account and data directly within the app by following these steps:

  1. Navigate to your Profile.
  2. Tap on Setting.
  3. Go to Account Centre.
  4. Select Manage Account.
  5. From there, select the option to delete your account and confirm.

Note: Once confirmed, your account, videos, comments, and all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.
