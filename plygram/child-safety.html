Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

PlyGram is a platform for creative expression, and we are deeply committed to maintaining a safe, positive, and secure environment for all our users. We strictly prohibit any content, activity, or behavior that exploits, harms, or endangers minors. Please note that PlyGram is intended for users who are at least 13 years of age.

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of child endangerment, including but not limited to:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any creation, sharing, uploading, or promotion of CSAM is strictly forbidden. Violators will face immediate permanent account termination, and the incident will be reported to the appropriate law enforcement authorities and reporting centers (such as NCMEC or local equivalents).
  • Grooming and Exploitation: Any attempt to solicit, groom, exploit, or inappropriately contact minors will result in an immediate ban.
  • Cyberbullying and Harassment: We do not tolerate the bullying, threatening, or harassment of minors (or any users) on our platform.

3. Content Moderation and User Controls

To ensure a safe environment, PlyGram utilizes both automated systems and manual moderation to detect and remove harmful content. Furthermore, we empower our community with robust in-app tools:

  • Flag / Report Content: Users can instantly report videos, comments, or accounts that violate our safety policies.
  • Block Users: Users can block anyone exhibiting inappropriate behavior, preventing any further contact or content visibility.

4. Reporting Safety Concerns

If you are a parent, guardian, or user and you encounter any content or behavior that violates our child safety standards, please use the in-app Report feature immediately. For urgent safety concerns, you can directly reach out to our dedicated support team.
