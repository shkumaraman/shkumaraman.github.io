PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which SHKUMARAMAN ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users ("User" or "you") of the PlyGram mobile application (the "Service"). This document outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTION

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve our Service.

  • Information You Provide: Account registration details (name, email address, date of birth, profile images), user-generated content (videos, comments, audio files), and communications with our support team.
  • Information Collected Automatically: Device identifiers (IP address, MAC address, OS type), usage metrics (session duration, interaction logs), and geographic location data (if permitted by device settings).
  • Financial Information: For Users participating in the PlyGram Monetization Program or utilizing our in-app promotion services, we collect necessary billing and payment details, processed securely through authorized third-party payment gateways.

3. USE OF INFORMATION

We utilize the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate account creation and authentication.
  • To deliver, personalize, and optimize content delivery (e.g., algorithmic feeds).
  • To process monetization earnings, issue payouts, and manage transaction records for promotional campaigns.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service, detect fraudulent activity, and ensure platform security.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. USER-GENERATED CONTENT (UGC) AND MODERATION

As a media-sharing platform, content uploaded to PlyGram is publicly accessible. We employ automated and manual moderation tools to monitor UGC. We reserve the right to retain, review, and delete any content that violates our Community Guidelines or applicable laws.

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose your information strictly under the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: To authorized third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (cloud storage, analytics, payment processing).
  • Legal Compliance: In response to lawful requests by public authorities, court orders, or legal processes.
  • Protection of Rights: To investigate fraud, security breaches, or potential violations of our Terms of Service.

6. ADVERTISING AND PROMOTED CONTENT

We display advertisements within the PlyGram app through a combination of our native in-app promotion features and third-party ad networks.

  • In-App Promotions (Custom Ads): PlyGram allows users to promote their own content natively on the platform. We may use your in-app activity and preferences to show you relevant promoted content. However, your personal data is never shared with the users running these promotions.
  • Third-Party Ads (Google AdMob): We also use Google AdMob as our primary third-party advertising partner. AdMob may collect information such as your device's advertising ID (GAID), IP address, and location to provide personalized advertisements. You can learn more by visiting the Google Privacy & Terms.
  • Opt-out: You may opt-out of personalized third-party advertising by visiting your device settings (e.g., Android Settings > Google > Ads > Opt-out of Ads Personalization).

7. DATA RETENTION AND SECURITY

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

8. ACCOUNT DELETION

We value your privacy and give you full control over your data.

  • In-App Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the PlyGram App by navigating to Profile > Setting > Account Centre > Manage Account, and from there you can select the option to delete your account.
  • Web/Email Request: If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at kumarsharma2580@gmail.com with your registered email address and the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 48 hours.
